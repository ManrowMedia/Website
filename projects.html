BLACK ROCK AQUATICS

Black Rock Aquatics originated from a love of aquascaping and bringing the beauty of healthy, thriving plants into more homes! As a direct to consumer seller of aquatic plants, Black Rock Aquatics inquired for print design services to provide a more premium customer experience and strengthen their brand image.

Together, we designed over 70 custom ‘Care Cards,’ one for each unique plant sold. Our partnership with Black Rock Aquatics turned simple plant care information into a premium customer experience — clear, consistent, and beautifully on-brand.
